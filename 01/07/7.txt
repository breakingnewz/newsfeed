Mike Pence Tells Juan Guaidó He Is the ‘Only Legitimate President’ of Venezuela 
https://www.wsj.com/articles/pence-tells-juan-guaido-he-is-the-only-legitimate-president-of-venezuela-11578343578?mod=hp_lead_pos3
Mike Pence, Juan Guaidó, Venezuela

WASHINGTON—Vice President Mike Pence spoke by phone with Venezuela’s Juan Guaidó and reiterated the U.S. position that Mr. Guaidó is the country’s “only legitimate” leader, a senior administration official said.
