Wuhan coronavirus: Citywide lockdown disrupts Lunar New Year travel plans of millions
https://edition.cnn.com/2020/01/23/china/wuhan-coronavirus-update-intl-hnk/index.html
China, virus

(CNN) - Authorities in China have enforced a partial lockdown of transport links in and out of the central city of Wuhan, disrupting the Lunar New Year travel plans of millions as part of efforts to contain a deadly coronavirus outbreak.
