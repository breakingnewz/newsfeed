Coronavirus Death Toll Rises in China as U.S. Reports Second Case
https://www.wsj.com/articles/spreading-chinese-coronavirus-death-toll-rises-as-more-cities-are-locked-down-11579860808?mod=hp_lead_pos2&mod=article_inline
China, virus

The tally of deaths and infections from the spread of the new coronavirus in China jumped again on Friday—with authorities canceling large public gatherings and locking down more cities—as the U.S. confirmed its second infection and the disease spread into Europe and Australia for the first time.
