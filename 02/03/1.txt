The Wuhan coronavirus death toll in mainland China has overtaken SARS

Hong Kong (CNN) - The number of Wuhan coronavirus deaths in mainland China has overtaken the 2003 severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) epidemic in the country, as Beijing injected billions of dollars into an economy hit by weeks of effective shutdowns to major cities.
