Artist uses 99 phones to trick Google into traffic jam alert
https://edition.cnn.com/style/article/artist-google-traffic-jam-alert-trick-scli-intl/index.html
Berlin, Google Maps, traffic jam

An artist in Berlin has tricked Google Maps into creating traffic jam alerts by pulling 99 phones -- with their location services on -- slowly around the German capital in a handcart.
