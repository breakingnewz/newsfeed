China halves tariffs on $75 billion worth of US goods, as coronavirus outbreak escalates
https://edition.cnn.com/2020/02/06/economy/china-tariffs-coronavirus/index.html
China, US, goods

Hong Kong/Beijing (CNN Business) - China announced on Thursday that it will halve tariffs on $75 billion worth of US imports, as the world's two largest economies continue to step back from a years-long trade war that has hurt both countries and dented global growth.
