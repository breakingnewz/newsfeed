Kirk Douglas, acclaimed actor, dead at 103
https://edition.cnn.com/2020/02/05/entertainment/kirk-douglas-obit/index.html
Kirk Douglas, death, longevity

(CNN) - Kirk Douglas, one of the great Hollywood leading men whose off-screen life was nearly as colorful as his on-screen exploits in movies like "Spartacus" and "Champion," has died, according to his son, actor Michael Douglas. He was 103.
